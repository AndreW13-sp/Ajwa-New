About Us

Ajwa House of Dry Fruits, is a brand developed and owned by Mr. Rizwan Patel & Azhar Patel. We started our first outlet in Kharghar, in 2014 and now we have 7+ outlets across INDIA. We brings you a collection of carefully selected foods from India and across the world. In our passionate search to offer food that is unique and full of health, we have ensured you get only the best products.

Our Products

Our products are well-loved for their quality and taste. We have gained expertise in fine Indian food products and food ingredients across categories - Dry Fruits, Dried Fruits, Dates, Sweets, Chocolates, Namkeen, Bakery Products, Mukhwaas, Spices, Gift Boxes and Groceries. We are sure you will find our quality products appetizing.

OUR MISSION

Our Mission is not only to earn money but also Ajwa wants to deliver the best quality healthy products towards our customers.

OUR VISION

Our Vision is to establish Ajwa store in every state of our country & globally so that every people can avail our quality products and keep themselves healthier.
